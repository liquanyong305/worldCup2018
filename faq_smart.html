What do I need to play World Cup 2018 Lottery?

1.Install Trust Wallet app

To play World Cup 2018 Lottery On your smartphone, you need to install Trust Wallet app.

2.Change to Ethereum Network

On Trust Wallet app, go to "Setting" → "Network", Then click Ethereum (ETH).

3.Visit world Cup 2018 Lottery

To use this DApps, ETH is required, Deposit ETH to Trust.

Let's specify the address of the account (wallet) prepared as the remittance destination.

You can display the address by selecting "Wallet" → "Receive". Copy the address with 'Copy Wallet's address' so you can use it to prevent typing errors.

4.Visit world Cup 2018 Lottery

On Trust Wallet app, Visit https://eth-world-cup.herokuapp.com/
